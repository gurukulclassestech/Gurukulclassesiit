Message from the Founder's Desk

Prof. Pradip Giri

Dear Students/Parents,

Every moment in life is a fresh beginning that brings with it the boundless joy of new possibilities, and the pleasure is compounded when you know that you are about to realize a lofty goal.

For many years, I have been constantly in touch with students, guiding them and addressing their difficulties in succeeding in competitive examinations like JEE (Main & Advance), NEET, AIIMS & U.P.M.T. I felt a need for a good coaching center to fulfill the requirements of students. Students need highly experienced, qualified faculty in all subjects who can guide them well, clear their doubts, provide them with the right type of printed notes, and make them do the much-needed exercises. More importantly, they should also provide proper direction. Students and parents have placed their confidence in me in the past, so I am hopeful to receive the same in my present venture. I have formed an excellent team of faculty who will give personal attention in all manners possible. I am fully conscious of the onerous responsibility I have been entrusted with.

When you join Giri sir's Gurukul Classes, you become part of a powerful force that propels you toward your goal. If you achieve a position among the rankers with my excellent guidance, then I will consider our efforts to have paid off.
