About Giri sir's Gurukul Classes

Our Vision

To provide each student an education in an academically excellent and individually responsive learning community.

We Believe That

All students must be challenged. High expectations result in high performance. Continuous improvement yields excellence. A supportive and secure environment is essential to learning. Trust and respect form the cornerstone of the Kota culture.

Goals

We will develop individual and academic responsibility. We will prepare all of our students to achieve academically excellent results. We will ensure a safe learning environment. We will give full benefit to the student for the fee they are paying. We will recruit, develop, and recognize highly qualified faculty. We will demonstrate responsibility, efficiency, and effectiveness in all operations.

Our Teaching Methodology

We have well-experienced and highly qualified faculty in a highly competitive environment. Our teaching includes highly relevant sets of questions with required depth of concept, regular tests every Sunday, classroom coaching, and monthly reports to all guardians.

Competitive Theory Notes and Exercise Sheets are provided.

What People Say About Us

Giri sir's Gurukul Classes provides excellent infrastructure and facilities to create an ideal ambiance for effective learning with caring and committed management and administration. Students are made to sit in the classroom in order of the ascending sequence of their ranks to enthuse the student with a competitive spirit at every step. The students' batches are reshuffled based on their performance in periodic tests, further inculcating the spirit of competition among the students.

Students Academic Performance & Evaluation Report (SAPER) Sheets are distributed to students and sent to parents 3-4 times a year. The SAPER includes marks secured by the student, Centre Rank, All Giri sir's Gurukul Classes Rank (ARR), Percentile, Test Percentage, and cumulative percentage. The performance is also available on the institute's website at Students' Corner.

Extra classes are planned and conducted as needed to complete the syllabus on time and remain aligned with course planning. Backup classes are arranged for specific students based on their performance to improve their performance levels. The institute also plays the role of a guardian by keeping a vigil on attendance and disciplinary activities of the students.

Motivation classes are conducted to ease stress and pressure from students' minds. Apart from routine periodic tests, several other tests are conducted to evaluate preparation levels for various objectives. Major Tests (MTs) revise the entire syllabus just before course completion, while Open Tests (OTs), JEE (Advanced) Preparatory Tests, JEE (Main), and Medical Preparatory Tests are conducted with students of Kota Classes as part of the All India Test Series (R-AITS) to intensify competition at a national level among JEE (Main/Advanced) and NEET aspirants.

Awareness is created through counseling among students for various Science & Mathematics Olympiads and other competitions that can help improve their confidence levels.
